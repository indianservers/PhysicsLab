## Known Limitations

| Area | Limitation |
|---|---|
| 3D physics | Phase 2 3D scenes are educational deterministic visualizations, not full physics engines. |
| Orbit visualization | Orbit/escape regimes are schematic and not to scale. |
| Collision animation | 3D collision scene shows conservation structure and arrows; it is not a time-stepped collision solver. |
| Hooke's law | Elastic-limit warning is visual/educational; material failure is not simulated. |

## Recommended Phase 3 Starting Point

Start with `force-and-pressure`, `fluid-pressure`, and `bernoulli-fluid-flow`, because they already have strong formulas and classroom-friendly apparatus concepts. Keep `buoyancy` as polish/QA unless inspection finds missing labels or fullscreen problems.
